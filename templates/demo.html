Demo & Working

How It's Working

  • Step 1: Vision Activation (Initialize Camera)

    • Starts the camera to capture real-time hand movements.

  • Step 2: Hand Tracker (Detect Hand & Finger Distance)

    • Perform a gesture in front of the camera.

  • Step 3: Recognition.

    • The system recognizes the gesture and translates it.

  • Step 3: Gesture Display

    • The result is displayed as text or triggers an action.

  • Step 4: Exit

    • Allows the user to stop, restart, or continue gesture recognition.
